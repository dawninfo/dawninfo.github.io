EXP & Levels

Dawn's levelling up system.

  • What is EXP? How can I gain them?

    • Dawn has its own EXP and Levelling feature. You can gain EXP points by talking in chat and doing the battle tower (/bt). The more EXP you gain, the more you level up. Levelling up unlocks many features like Custom avatars, profile customization, global declares for your room etc.

  • What's all this on the exp page mean?

    • Custom Avatars are pretty self explanatory. But the other things, such as Nature, Item, Personality, etc are all characteristics you can set, just like in the games. These characteristics will appear on your user profile. Check it by typing /profile into the chat. Additionally, you can check any user's profile by typing their name after /profile. You can also unlock the ability to choose your own profile background and profile music. If you atleast at Level 5, you can also get daily draw awards like Pet eggs, PSGO Cards, monies, exp and even bucks!
