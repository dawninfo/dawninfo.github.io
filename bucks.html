Bucks:

Dawn's Economy System.

  • What are Bucks and how can I earn them?

    • Dawn's official currency is Bucks. You can earn them by winning Official room tournaments (or by being runner up). Bucks can be used to buy Azuremons, PSGO cards and every time in the Global Shop (/shop) or from room shops (/roomshop). You can also get your friends transfer you a pet or azuremon in exchange of bucks. And if you like taking risks, you can spend your bucks by gambling in Casino by using /startdice (bucks).

    Use /atm [user] to see how rich a certain user is. The economy leaderboard can be viewed by using /richestusers.
