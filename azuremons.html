Create your favorite pokemon with almost endless customization of moves and abilities! Guide made by Aeova.

  • What are Azuremons?

    • Azuremons are custom pokemon made by other users by spending bucks , azuremons usually have different abilities, moves and nicknames that can be used in the azuremons format.

  • How do I check how many kinds of azuremons does a certain user have?

    • /azuremons showcase [user] shows a list of azuremons [user] owns.

  • How do I make an Azuremon?

    • 1) First go to any chatroom and type /azuremons.

    Then you will see this:


    Click the ‘+’ button to make a new azuremon

    Here you will see a list of all available pokemon that you can buy. The prices of each pokemon are also listed.



    Click on the pokemon you desire to buy. Make sure you have enough bucks for the purchase.

    You can see a list of moves and abilities it naturally gets before buying it.

    There you will see a button asking you to confirm your purchase.



    Click it and now you have you have your very own azuremon. Congratulations!~
